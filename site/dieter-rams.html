About Dieter Rams

Dieter Rams was born in Wiesbaden Germany in 1932. He was strongly influenced by his grandfather who was a carpenter. Prompted by his friend he applied for a job at the german electrical products company Braun in 1955. During his 40 years at Braun, Dieter Rams produced and oversaw 500 innovative products as chief of design. Dieter Rams is famously one of the first designers who strived to be environmentally friendly before it was popular. He believed "design can and must maintain its contribution towards protecting and sustaining the environment."
