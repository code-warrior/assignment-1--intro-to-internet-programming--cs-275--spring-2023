Dieter Rams' Ten Principles for Good Design

Is Innovative.    Makes a product useful.    Is aesthetic.    Makes a product understandable.    Is unobtrusive.
        Is honest.    Is long-lasting.    Is thorough down to the last detail.    Is environmentally friendly.    Is as little design as possible.

5. Is unobtrusive.

Products fulfilling a purpose are like tools. They are neither decorative objects nor works of art. Their design should therefore be both neutral and restrained, to leave room for the user's self-expression.
